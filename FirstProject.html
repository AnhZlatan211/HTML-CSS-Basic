Haworthia Limifolia

This plant is native to South Africa, and grows as a compact asterisk, only 12cm in diameter.

Because of its size, you should always plant it in a pot, or in a pot with other small succulents, such as Gasteria or Lithops.

The leaves are thick and hard, which may be green or patchy (green and yellow). Its flowers appear in upright inflorescences up to 35cm tall. It blooms in spring and early summer.

It is a small succulent plant that is perfect for decorating terraces and balconies, and even for filling gaps on tables that have been left empty. The maintenance of this plant is very simple, as it tolerates drought and high temperatures, and It is generally free from diseases or pests.
